Project Alpha

Project Alpha is an open source project which serves as an internet based dating platform. In order to protect your privacy please do use a pseudonym instead of your real name for registration. A valid email address which will not be published elsewhere is the only thing which is required for registration.

Features

Transparancy

Would you like to understand the criteria for finding a significant other instead of relying on team of experienced psychologists? If so your have just found the right place. On contrast to many other dating sides based on 10 simple and directly questions you are able to clearly comprehend who matches to your character and who will not.

The way the algorithms behind this side work can be tracked down to the last detail because there are open source. This has also the advantage that third party application like in example smart phone apps can easily created. An iPhone app is already under development.

Because we do not earn any additional revenue based on your usage, there is no commercial interest to keep you logged into this side as long as possible. Just the opposite. You are entitled to switch to whatever network services works well for you in case you find somebody interesting here.

No fees, no ads

This side disclaims completely advertisement and fees. There is in example no expensive "premium account" which are forced to conclude because you are not able to write messages to some of the users otherwise. Futhermore you are not convert yourself into a product by trading your personal data or expose you to unwanted commercials.

Maybe your are asking yourself how this side is making money? The answer is simple. It is the same way like Wikipedia, Linux and other open source projects do it. These projects are founded on donations and the work of volunteers. Maybe there is another question which you may better ask: Why are commercial vendors of a dating platform which is relatively simple to implement such expensive?

Best in class privacy protection

You are explitly adviced not to use any real name on this platform to keep you personal data secure. All you need to use this side is a valid email address. If you like you can create one excplicitly for accessing this side.

On contrast to most dating sides we do not store pictures on our servers directly. Nevertheless you can put pictures to your profile or messages by delegating to an external service. You have full control about your pictures in this way and you do not need to worry about any potential misuse. In practise procedure of linking pictures in is dead simple. Just drag and drop them with the mouse from another side.
